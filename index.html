Your Loan Questions

Get clear answers about our loan process and requirements. All operations comply with BSP Lending Regulations.

Can't find your question? Call our support at +63 2 8888-8888 or email help@vplus.ph

What documents do I need to apply?
You'll need:
  • - Valid Philippine ID (UMID/Passport/Driver's License)
  • - Proof of income (3 months payslips or bank statements)
  • - Proof of address (utility bill less than 3 months old)
No collateral required for loans below ₱50,000.
How long does loan approval take?
Most applications are approved within 5 minutes during business hours. Once approved, funds will be disbursed:
  • - Within 5 minutes for GCash/Maya users
  • - 1-3 business days for bank account users
We process loans 24/7, including weekends.
What happens if I can't pay on time?
Contact our support team at least 3 days before due date. Options include:
  • 1. Grace period of 3 days (no additional fees)
  • 2. Loan restructuring with extended term
  • 3. Partial payment arrangements
Late fee is 5% of due amount (max ₱500/month) as per BSP guidelines.
