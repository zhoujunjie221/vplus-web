Our Credentials

Regulated Financial Services

Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation (Vplus) is a BSP Licensed Lender and is regulated by the Securities and Exchange Commission (Registration No. CS201918401) and authorized by the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) under Certificate of Authority No. 3158.

Our digital lending services operate in strict compliance with:

  • BSP Circular No. 942 (Electronic Financial Services)
  • Republic Act No. 11765 (Consumer Protection Act)
  • BSP Liquidity Requirements for EMI operators

Vplus leverages cutting-edge financial technology to deliver secure and accessible credit solutions while adhering to the highest standards of consumer protection mandated by Philippine financial regulators.
