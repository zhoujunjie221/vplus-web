Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-06-23

Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App") to access loan services in the Philippines. By using our App, you agree to the terms of this Policy.

1. DATA WE COLLECT

We may collect the following categories of data:

A. Personal Information

  • Identification Documents: Government-issued IDs, selfie verification.
  • Personal Details: Name, gender, educational background, marital status.
  • Professional or employment Details: Company information, industry, years of working.
  • Contact Details: Email address, phone number, address and emergency contacts.
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, credit history, income information.

B. Device & Technical Information

  • Device Data: Device type, device name, device brand, operating system (type, version), unique device identifiers (Android ID, MAC address, GAID), Wi-Fi information (Wi-Fi name, carrier name, Wi-Fi connection status (Yes/No)), Local IP address, screen resolution, root status (Yes/No), whether you are using an emulator (Yes/No), browser type and settings, device storage capacity (total capacity and remaining capacity), device memory data (total space and usage space), emulator usage status, app version.
  • Location Data (Rough location information): GPS address, latitude, longitude.
  • Device Memory Data: Total memory space and usage memory space.

C. Behavioral Data

  • Borrowing history, repayment behavior, and interactions with our customer support.

D. Third-Party and External Data

We may also collect data from external sources, including but not limited to:

  • Your Submission: Information you directly provide (e.g., account updates, loan applications).
  • Account Analysis: Behavioral insights derived from how you use our services.
  • Public Registries: Publicly available data from government databases or registries.
  • Authorities: Information shared by government agencies, regulators, or law enforcement.
  • Telecom Providers: Mobile network operator data (e.g., carrier details) with your consent.
  • Third Parties: Data obtained from partners, service providers, or publicly accessible platforms.

Key Conditions:

  • We collect such data only with your explicit consent or where permitted by law (e.g., regulatory requirements).
  • You warrant that all submitted information is accurate and not misleading.
  • Vplus reserves the right to request additional documentation to verify your identity or data.

2. PURPOSES OF DATA COLLECTION

We use your data for the following lawful bases:

  1. Loan Processing: Authenticate your identity to comply with Know Your Customer (KYC) and anti-money laundering (AML) regulations.
  2. Fraud Prevention: Detect and prevent fraudulent transactions or account takeovers.
  3. Credit Assessment: Conduct credit checks to evaluate your financial credibility. And share credit data with authorized financial institutions for joint risk assessments.
  4. Financial Profiling: Analyze your financial needs, spending patterns, and risk profile to tailor loan products.
  5. Account Management: Maintain and secure your accounts, including transactions, security protocols, and facility access.
  6. Data Analytics: Generate insights to improve products, services, and operational efficiency.
  7. Credit History Management: Retain and reference credit records for current or future loan evaluations.
  8. Service Improvement: Enhance app functionality, user experience, and personalized loan offers.
  9. Customer Support: Address inquiries, resolve disputes, send updates/reminders, and handle complaints.
  10. Marketing & Promotions: Send transaction updates, reminders, and promotional offers (with your consent).
  11. Debt Recovery: Enforce repayment obligations and recover overdue payments.
  12. Record Verification: Cross-check data accuracy with third parties to ensure reliability.
  13. Experience Enhancement: Optimize app functionality, user experience, and business processes.
  14. Business Operations: Support financial planning, risk monitoring, audits, and decision-making.
  15. Legal Compliance: Respond to subpoenas, regulatory requests, or court orders.

3. THE WAY OF DATA COLLECTION

If you decide to open an Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation Account and provide your personal information to use our services (such as submitting loan applications, contacting us for support, or other related purposes), we will collect, process, and share various types of information. Rest assured, we will only retain and use this information for as long as necessary to fulfill the stated purposes or as required by law.

4. PERMISSIONS AND PURPOSE

To deliver seamless services and ensure account security, Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation requires specific device permissions. Below is a detailed breakdown of their purposes and safeguards:

A. Internet Access

Purpose:

  • Enable data transmission between your device and our servers for service functionality (e.g., loan applications, updates).
  • Ensure real-time updates and secure communication.

B. Network State Check (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)

Purpose: Monitor network connectivity to optimize app performance and stability.

C. Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)

Purpose:

  • Send urgent alerts (e.g., transaction confirmations, security reminders).
  • Deliver personalized promotions and service updates.

D. Wi-Fi State Access (ACCESS_WIFI_STATE / CHANGE_WIFI_STATE)

Purpose:

  • Optimize network performance by analyzing Wi-Fi connectivity.
  • Automatically switch to more reliable networks for uninterrupted service.

E. Location Access (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)

Purpose:

  • Provide location-based services.
  • Enhance fraud prevention by cross-referencing geographic data.

F. Camera Access

Purpose:

  • Capture ID documents and live selfies for KYC verification.
  • Enable document scanning and biometric authentication.

5. DELETION OR MODIFICATION OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

A. Modification of Personal Information

You may update or correct your personal information by:

  • Request Method: Submit an email to customer.support@vastness-ph.com specifying the data to be modified (e.g., contact details, address).
  • Processing Timeline: We will review and update your data within 5 business days of receiving the request.
  • Verification: For critical information (e.g., government ID), identity verification documents may be required to ensure account security.

B. Deletion of Personal Information

You may request deletion of your personal data through:

  1. Voluntary Deletion Request:
    • Email customer.support@vastness-ph.com with a clear request to delete all or part of your data.
    • Verification: Identity verification (e.g., ID photo) and a reason for deletion may be required.
    • Processing Timeline: Data will be purged from all systems within 15 natural days after verification.
  2. Manual Deletion Feature:
    • Use the “Account Settings” section in the Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation app to delete your account and data.
    • Irreversible Action: Deleted data cannot be recovered. Note that some data may be retained per legal requirements (e.g., transaction records).

C. Exceptions

The following data cannot be deleted under this policy:

  • Legally mandated records (e.g., loan contracts, repayment history).
  • Data shared with third-party service providers (requires third-party cooperation for deletion).

6. DATA DISCLOSURE

Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation takes your privacy seriously and will only share Personal Information as explicitly permitted under this Privacy Policy. We may disclose your data to the following categories of recipients only for the Permitted Purposes outlined in Section 2 (Purposes of Data Collection) and/or as required by law:

A. Internal Affiliates and Service Providers

  1. Affiliates/Subsidiaries: Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation’s holding companies, subsidiaries, affiliates, and associates for operational, financial, or service-related functions.
  2. Service Providers: Contractors, professional advisers (e.g., legal, audit), debt collection agencies, and third-party vendors processing data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

B. Credit and Financial Institutions

  1. Credit Bureaus: National and local credit reporting agencies (e.g., credit bureaus, insurers, or rating agencies) for risk assessment, loan underwriting, or fraud prevention.
  2. Financial Partners: Financial service providers engaged to deliver products/services directly to you (e.g., payment gateways, loan disbursement platforms).

C. Legal and Regulatory Entities

  1. Authorities: Government regulators (e.g., SEC Philippines, Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas), law enforcement, or courts to comply with legal obligations, investigations, or court orders.
  2. Guarantors/Security Providers: Entities providing guarantees or collateral for your loans.

D. Business Partners and Rights Holders

  1. Strategic Partners: Third parties collaborate with Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation for specific products, promotions, or services (e.g., co-branded financial products).
  2. Assignees/Transferees: Entities to whom we transfer rights or obligations under our agreements with you (e.g., mergers, acquisitions, or debt assignments).

E. Other Legitimate Recipients

  1. Enforcement Agents: Persons/entities assisting in the enforcement or preservation of Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation’s contractual or legal rights.
  2. Authorized Third Parties: Any party explicitly authorized by you through written consent or enabled via account settings (e.g., sharing data with trusted apps via integration).

7. DATA RETENTION

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy or as required by Philippine law. Below are the retention periods for specific categories of data:

A. Personal, Behavioral Data and Third-Party and External Data

  • Personal Information: Retained for 3 years after your last transaction via Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation or immediately deleted upon account closure.
  • Behavioral Data: Retained for 3 years after your last transaction via Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation or immediately deleted upon account closure.
  • Third-Party and External Data: Retained for 3 years after your last transaction via Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation or immediately deleted upon account closure.

B. Device and Technical Data

  • Device Data:
    • Active Records: Retained for 90 days.
    • Backup Records: Retained for up to 3 years for fraud prevention and risk analysis.
    • Deleted immediately upon account closure.
  • Location Data:
    • Active Records: Retained for 90 days.
    • Backup Records: Retained for up to 3 years for fraud prevention and risk analysis.
    • Deleted immediately upon account closure.
  • Device Memory Data:
    • Active Records: Retained for 90 days.
    • Backup Records: Retained for up to 3 years for fraud prevention and risk analysis.
    • Deleted immediately upon account closure.

C. Legal and Regulatory Exceptions

Data required to comply with Philippine law (e.g., Anti-Money Laundering Act, SEC reporting) will be retained for the minimum period mandated by regulators (typically 5–7 years).

8. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER THE DATA PRIVACY ACT (DPA)

As a user of Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation, you are entitled to the following rights under Philippine law. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at customer.support@vastness-ph.com or submit a formal request via our Customer Service:

A. Right to Be Informed

You have the right to know:

  • What personal data we collect about you.
  • How we use, share, or store your data (as outlined in Sections 2 and 7 of this Policy).
  • The purposes and legal bases for processing your data.

B. Right to Access

You may request a copy of your personal data held by Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation, including:

  • Copies of loan applications, transaction records, and account details.
  • Documentation of how your data has been shared with third parties.

C. Right to Rectification

You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data, such as:

  • Outdated contact information.
  • Errors in financial or identification documents.

D. Right to Erasure (Blocking)

You may request deletion of your personal data unless legally retained (e.g., for tax, audit, or ongoing legal proceedings). Examples include:

  • Closing your account and requesting removal of associated data.
  • Deleting stored chat history or unnecessary behavioral data.

E. Right to Restrict Processing

You can object to processing your data for specific purposes, such as:

  • Direct marketing (e.g., promotional emails).
  • Profiling for credit scoring or behavioral analysis.

F. Right to Data Portability

You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format (e.g., CSV, JSON) to transfer it to another financial service provider.

G. Right to File a Complaint

You are entitled to raise objections at any time regarding the handling of your Personal Information, in accordance with Philippine law, as well as the processing of your personal data for the Permitted Purpose. For any inquiries, requests, concerns, complaints, or the exercise of your rights concerning the utilization of your Personal Information, you may reach out to Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation. Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation assures you that your complaint will be promptly acknowledged, and a tentative timeframe will be provided for the company's response to your concern.

Please note that the enumerated rights and wording thereof is not an exact copy of those indicated in the Data Privacy Act. Please check The Data Privacy Act for the exact list and rights you may have as a data subject. All the rights mentioned in such law are inherently incorporated into this Privacy Policy and may be freely exercised by the data subject.

9. PROTECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

Philippine Vastness Lending Corporation is committed to safeguarding your personal information through a multi-layered security framework that aligns with Philippine law and international best practices (e.g., ISO 27001). Below are the key safeguards we implement:

A. Data Encryption

  • In Transit: All data exchanged between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3 (Transport Layer Security).
  • At Rest: Sensitive data (e.g., passwords, financial details) is stored using AES-256 encryption.

B. Access Controls

  • Role-Based Permissions: Employees and contractors are granted access strictly based on job roles (e.g., customer support vs. system admins).
  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): Mandatory for accessing internal systems containing personal data.

C. Network Security

  • Firewalls & Intrusion Detection: Deployed to monitor and block unauthorized access attempts.
  • Regular Penetration Testing: Conducted quarterly to identify and patch vulnerabilities.

D. Data Backup & Recovery

  • Backup Frequency:
    • Active Data: Daily incremental backups.
    • Long-Term Retention: Weekly full backups stored in geographically dispersed secure facilities.
    • Retention Period: Backup data is retained for 3 years for disaster recovery purposes, in line with SEC requirements.

E. Employee Training

  • Annual Security Training: Covers phishing prevention, data handling protocols, and incident reporting.
  • Role-Specific Workshops: For IT staff on secure coding practices and third-party vendor risk management.

F. Third-Party Vendor Management

  • Contractual Obligations: Service providers (e.g., cloud hosts, payment gateways) must comply with our Data Processing Addendum (DPA).
  • Audit Rights: Vplus reserves the right to audit third parties for compliance with security standards.

G. Incident Response

  • Breach Protocol: Immediate containment, forensic analysis, and notification to affected users and regulators (e.g., SEC, BSP) within 72 hours of discovery.
  • User Communication: Transparent updates via email/SMS if your data is compromised.

H. Continuous Improvement

  • Risk Assessments: Bi-annual evaluations of security measures to address emerging threats (e.g., AI-driven fraud patterns).
  • Policy Updates: Regular revisions to reflect technological advancements and regulatory changes.

However, despite our substantial investment in protecting your personal information, ultimately no institution can guarantee its absolute.
